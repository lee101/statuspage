AI APIs without making your stack messy

For teams adding AI features, the common failure mode is not the first demo. It is cost drift, provider latency, missing fallbacks, unclear privacy posture, and no way to explain an outage when a model provider has problems.

Text-Generator.io is the broad API surface for text, vision, and speech workflows. Open Paths is the model-routing idea for provider choice, latency, throughput, and fallback control.

Useful pairing: monitor your AI endpoint, your model router, and your upstream provider status separately. Then your status page can say whether the issue is your app, routing, or a third-party AI provider.
