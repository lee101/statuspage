Fast prototypes, fiddles, and simulated sites

Sometimes the fastest way to explain an idea is a working browser prototype. Small tools with save, run, share, and edit loops can cut days from feedback cycles.

Web Fiddle is the lightweight in-browser editor pattern. Site Sim pushes further into AI website simulation with preview, HTML, CSS, JS, files, details, and agent-style editing modes.

Use your status page to separate the editor, preview runtime, asset storage, and share links. Users can tolerate a degraded editor better when existing shared work still loads.
